## 📌 Project Overview

## 🔍 Key Insights

1. **West region leads in both sales and profit** — generating the highest revenue (~$0.5M+ in sales) and the most profit ($108K), making it the most valuable market.

2. **Steady revenue growth from 2014 to 2017** — sales have grown consistently year-on-year, indicating a healthy and expanding business.

3. **East region is a strong performer** — with $92K in profit, East is a close second and should be prioritized for retention strategies.

4. **Central region underperforms** — despite reasonable sales volume, Central records the lowest profit ($40K), suggesting high discounting or cost inefficiencies.

5. **Some sub-categories are operating at a loss** — certain sub-categories show negative profit margins, dragging overall profitability down.

---

## ✅ Recommendations

1. **Investigate Central region performance** — conduct a discount and cost audit to identify why profit margins are thin despite decent sales.

2. **Double down on the West** — allocate more marketing and inventory resources to the top-performing region to maximize returns.

3. **Review loss-making sub-categories** — consider reducing discounts, repricing, or discontinuing products that consistently generate negative profit.

4. **Leverage the growth trend** — with 4 years of consistent sales growth, the business is well-positioned to scale; focus on high-margin categories (Technology, Office Supplies) for expansion.

5. **South region has untapped potential** — it has the lowest sales volume but is showing profitability; targeted campaigns here could unlock growth.
